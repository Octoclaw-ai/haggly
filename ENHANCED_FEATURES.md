# Haggly Enhanced Features - Psychology-Driven Negotiation

## Core Value Proposition
"Navigate every step of the sales cycle with confidence - whether you're buying or selling"

## New App Structure

### Main Navigation
1. **I'M BUYING** - Buyer's toolkit and scripts
2. **I'M SELLING** - Seller's responses and strategies  
3. **SITUATION ANALYZER** - Context-aware guidance
4. **PSYCHOLOGY INSIGHTS** - Why these techniques work

### Pre-Purchase Phase
- **Research Assistant**: "Help me determine fair market value"

- **Red Flag Detector**: Warning signs to watch for
- **Negotiation Range Calculator**: Based on condition, market, urgency

### During Negotiation
- **Guard Down Alerts**: Recognize when you're being upsold
- **Response Scripts**: Context-aware comebacks
- **Walk-Away Calculator**: Know when to say no
- **Body Language Guide**: Read seller signals

### Car Buying Specific
- **Dealership vs Private**: Different strategies for each

- **Trade-In Tactics**: When and how to mention your trade
- **Timing Strategies**: Best times to buy for maximum leverage
